Frequently Asked Questions

What are system actions?

System actions allow Centinela to not just read your data, but also write back to your systems. Create invoices, update CRM records, send notifications—all through natural language commands.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time and only pay the difference. Your data and configurations will transfer seamlessly.

What's included in dedicated support?

Dedicated support includes weekly strategy meetings, direct Slack/Teams access to our team, custom training, and priority response times.

How long does setup take?

Most companies are up and running within 3 weeks. Complex Enterprise deployments may take 4-6 weeks depending on the number of integrations.
